ABOUT ME
My journey as a front-end developer began with a
passion for creating visually appealing and
user-friendly websites. With a strong foundation in
HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I honed my skills in
frameworks like React, Vue, and Angular, and
embraced modern design tools such as Tailwind
CSS. Over the years, I've developed a
reputation for delivering top-notch web
designs, incorporating features like
animations, smooth scrolling, and responsive
layouts. My focus on efficiency and
high-quality work has enabled me to excel in
e-commerce front-end development, consistently
exceeding client expectations.
